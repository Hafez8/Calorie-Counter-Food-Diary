Did You Know?

Did you know that your body burns calories just by eating?

This phenomenon is known as the Thermic Effect of Food (TEF), which refers to the energy expenditure required for digestion, absorption, and disposal of ingested nutrients. Not all foods are processed equally by the body, and the TEF can vary based on the macronutrient composition of the food consumed.
